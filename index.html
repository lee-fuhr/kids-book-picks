How we choose our picks

We do not run a testing lab or time children’s reading sessions. Instead, we rely on the long-term reputation of each title, culling feedback from parent communities, school librarians, and professional reviewers like School Library Journal and The Horn Book. We pay special attention to what owners report after years of use: which spines crack, which pages get dog-eared, and which stories truly get requested again and again. We also track which books appear on recommended reading lists from major literacy organizations (e.g., the American Library Association, Reading Rockets). Our picks reflect a consensus of experienced voices, not one editor’s opinion. If a book has stood the test of a decade, or longer, and continues to win over new generations, it earns a spot on our list.
